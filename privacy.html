Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Voidstrike ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Voidstrike mobile game application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (IDFA/IDFV on iOS), and IP address.
  • Gameplay Data: In-game progress, session duration, level completion, and in-app purchase history.
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction events, impressions, and click-through data collected by our advertising partners.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the game and its features
  • To display relevant advertisements through our ad network partners
  • To analyse usage patterns and improve the game experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Advertising partners: We share device identifiers and ad interaction data with AppLovin MAX and its demand-side partners solely to serve and measure advertisements.
  • Service providers: Trusted third parties who assist us in operating the Service, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Advertising Partners & Opt-Out

We use AppLovin MAX to serve in-game advertisements. AppLovin and its demand partners may collect and use data to serve targeted ads. AppLovin's privacy policy is available at applovin.com/privacy.

To opt out of interest-based advertising by AppLovin, visit applovin.com/optout.

On iOS, we request your permission before accessing the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalised. For attribution measurement, we use Apple's SKAdNetwork framework, which provides aggregate campaign data without sharing user-level identifiers.

Data shared with AppLovin and its demand partners may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. Such transfers are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards as required by applicable law.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. Gameplay save data is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of targeted advertising via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or directly at applovin.com/optout
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

7. GDPR — Legal Basis for Processing (EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests: Operating the game, fraud prevention, and analytics.
  • Consent: Serving personalised advertisements (via the ATT prompt on iOS). You may withdraw consent at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Legal obligation: Complying with applicable laws and regulations.

8. CCPA — California Residents

We do not sell personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information (not applicable here as we do not sell data). To exercise your rights, contact us at nico.frei@etik.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Voidstrike is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Third-Party SDKs

The Service includes the following third-party SDK. It has its own privacy policy governing use of data:

  • AppLovin MAX SDK — ad mediation and monetisation (privacy policy)

11. Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing nico.frei@etik.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days. Note that gameplay save data is stored only on your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at: nico.frei@etik.com
