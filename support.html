Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my progress?

Your progress is saved locally on your device. If you reinstall the game on the same device, your save should restore automatically. Transferring progress between devices is not currently supported.

The game is crashing — what should I do?

Try restarting your device and launching the game again. If the issue persists, email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of when the crash occurs. We'll investigate as a priority.

Why am I seeing ads?

Voidstrike is free to play and supported by advertising through AppLovin MAX. Ads allow us to keep the game free without paywalls on the core experience. You can opt out of personalised ads via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or at applovin.com/optout.

I found a bug — how do I report it?

Please email us with as much detail as possible: what happened, what you expected, your device model and iOS version, and if possible a screenshot or screen recording. Bug reports are greatly appreciated.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Voidstrike does not require an account to play. All your progress is stored locally on your device.

Is the game available on Android?

Currently Voidstrike is iOS only. Android support may come in a future update.
